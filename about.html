About the Project

Many high school students run into a dilemma upon graduation. They don’t know where to even begin when trying to select a college major. And it is not just graduating high school students; according to the National Center for Education Studies, 30% of college students in undergraduate programs had changed their major at least once and 10% of undergraduate students changed their major two or more times.

In order to help these student make better, well informed decisions, our group has set out to create a tool to aid in this decision making process.
